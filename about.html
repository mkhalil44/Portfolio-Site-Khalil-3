About

My Story

Hi! I'm Marcelle, a UI designer with a background in education and behavioral sciences. I've always had an interest in human behavior, which led me to receive a BA in Anthropology at UCLA and a Master's Degree in Science Education at UC Irvine. I worked as a high school science teacher for several years, which taught me how to clearly communicate my goals and expectations to a diverse group of young people, in addition to exposing me to a variety of education apps. My diverse background has provided me with the necessary tools to excel as a UI/ UX designer. My time spent with students and affinity for empathizing with people has helped me to create user-centric designs.
